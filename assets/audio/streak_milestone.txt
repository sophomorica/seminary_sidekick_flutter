## Sound description
A warm, bell-like ascending chime — three quick notes resolving upward —
played when a study streak hits a milestone (7, 14, 30 days). Reverent but
satisfying: think "small celebration in a chapel", not "slot machine". A
touch of soft reverb is fine here; this one is allowed to feel special since
it fires rarely.

## Where to source it
- freesound.org — search "achievement chime warm", "bell arpeggio short", "gentle success harp"
- Pixabay audio — "achievement bell", "level complete chime"
- Zapsplat — Sound Effects → Musical → harp/bell stingers

## Expected duration and format
~400–700ms, 44.1kHz .wav, mono is fine.
